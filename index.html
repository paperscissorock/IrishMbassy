This Irish-owned and Irish-run sports bar is THE place for whatever your game is.

 

If you kick it, throw it, hit it or whatever, it's on at the Irish Mbassy. No one else can show as many sports channels as we can on 10 big screen plasma tv's on three floors. Our bars are stocked with many local beers to choose from, as well as international brands, including bottled and on-tap varieties. Our extensive kitchen menu here caters for all appetites, featuring many bar favourites such as wings, nachos, hamburgers, curries, salads & sandwiches as well as full steak dinners and traditional Irish dishes.

 

We are located right in the very center of town, just off the main square, so you'll have no problem finding us. So the next time you are here in Krakow, drop by, grab a pint, relax and watch the match with us!
